Trauma Focused Equine Assisted Psychotherapy Workshop

Workshop is anchored in the work of experts in the field of trauma, including Dr. Bruce Perry, Dr. Bessel van der Kolk, & Dr. Frank Putnam. You will learn to view trauma as a state change disorder, and how to implement equine assisted interventions to heal the traumatized brain. You will understand how the physiology and psychology of the horse helps clients learn functional ways to emotionally regulate.
